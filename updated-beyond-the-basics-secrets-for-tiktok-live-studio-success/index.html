4. Quality and Objectivity

Translate live video in a way that maintains the original quality and meaning of the content. Translators should strive to provide high-quality translations. In certain movies, you might have observed biased translations of voiceovers and subtitles. Its ethically not allowed, and the presenters have faced many legal actions.
