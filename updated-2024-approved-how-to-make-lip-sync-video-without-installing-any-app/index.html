EnablingiPhone t ext to s peech function is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to harness the power of auditory content consumption:

Step 3

The icon associated with “Spoken Content” is usually characterized by a speaker or text bubble icon accompanied by some text. Step 5

This action will summon a tool known as the “selector tool,” which is represented by a blue, yellow and any other color bar. The selector tool empowers you to highlight text by dragging its handles located at both ends.
